The News

Nationwide Critical Alert test happening on Monday at 2PM

Many of you probably already know this, but there will be a national Critical Alert test of the AusAlert system on Monday at 2PM (Melbourne time). It's the first time we will be receiving "cell broadcast" warnings using the Common Alerting Protocol direct to smartphones. Unlike SMS alerts, these alerts override any silent or do not disturb settings on your phone, and only work on iOS 26.4 or newer or Android 12 or newer. The devices don't even need a SIM installed, as long as the radio is on, you'll get the alert. More info on the AusAlert website.

Codeberg to ban cryptocurrency and/or vibe-coded related projects

Popular German, non-profit and community-led Github alternative Codeberg has taken the kinda unusual step of disallowing both vibe-coded projects and cryptocurrency related projects from being hosted on their platform. They asked the community and the community voted in favour. We will get more details in a blog post by the Codeberg leaders on stuff like what they determine to be a cryptocurrency project versus a project that simply uses cryptocurrency, or what exactly constitutes vibe-coding and how they'll deal with it, but it's a strong stance from the Codeberg community, which seems a lot more ideals/values based than GitHub.

I liked teaching sex workers about computers and the internet

Today was my last day at Assembly Four, where I worked for a few years mostly writing blog posts about the internet, computers and cybersecurity for sex workers. Most of it is available to read on this NSFW blog, published under the pseudonym Ada Hamilton (Ada Lovelace & Margaret Hamilton!). As far as writing gigs go, it was awesome getting paid well to write for an audience that would otherwise not give a shit about "boring" topics like Yubikeys, how a VPN works, why end-to-end encryption is amazing, or trying to explain topics like data collection and large language models. For the first time in the 11 year history of The Sizzle, it is now my only job!
